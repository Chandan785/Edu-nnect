Faculink is a web platform designed to enhance communication and collaboration between students and faculty members. Built using Next.js, it enables features like faculty profiles, student queries, academic announcements, document sharing, and meeting scheduling. The goal is to streamline academic interactions in an organized and accessible digital environment.
