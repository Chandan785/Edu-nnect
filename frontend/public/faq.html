Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the EduPortal.

You can update your profile by logging into your dashboard, clicking the "Settings" button in the sidebar, and modifying your details in the pop-up modal. Your changes are saved instantly.

Your attendance records can be accessed from your main dashboard by clicking on the "View Attendance" card. This will show you a detailed breakdown by subject.

You can search for teacher profiles and view their contact information or office hours. Additionally, you can use the internal messaging system to send them a direct message.
